SD Friendly Plants

The best suitable plants for San Diego weather!


1. Star Jasmines

These petite flowers are a flexible addition to your garden. They can be left as a vine or spread out as bushes. These flowers are not only cute but also have a fragrant smell. While low maintenance (needs regular water and light shade), it is a great way to cover up unsightly things you do not want the public eye to view.

Purchase your Star Jasmines at Home Depot for $20!
Home Depot Star Jasmine


2. Dwarf Olive Tree

Nicknamed Little Ollie, these little trees are a cute decoration for your garden. This plant adapts well to many environments and is a flexible plant, whether you decide to leave it in a pot or plant it in the ground. If you leave it in a pot, remember to regularly water it, but if not, these plants need very little water (once a month). Dwarf olive treees thrive well in our coastal area since it can tolerate salt!

Purchase your Little Ollie at Lowes for $30!
Lowes Dwarf Olive Tree


3. Pittosporum

Pittosporum adapts extremely well, especially in Southern california. It can grow on many types of soil and can grow very easily to a tall height. Its dense leaves work well as another cover-up plant and can range in many colors. This plant also has tiny flowers that smell like orange blossoms and will be a beautiful addition to your garden.

Purchase your pittosporum at Home Depot for $40.
Home Depot Pittosporum



4. Aloe

Aloe is a form of succulent and grows well in dry conditions, perfect for San Diegans. This plant can grow anywhere, as long as there is a slightly more shady area. Luckily, this plant does not require much water (you should be more aware of watering it too much than not at all). This plant also has many health benefits (the insides can be used to treat sunburns and such)!

Purchase your aloe at Home Depot for $15!
Home Depot Aloe

5. Lavender

Lavender is one of the best additions you can possibly have in your garden. Here are some reasons if you still aren't convinced by the picture: Lavender is extremely beautiful and can add color to your garden. They also release a nice scent that can help when you want to sleep, are stressed, or have a headache. This plant is also low maintenance for San Diegans as it requires more than 6 hours of full sunlight per day, something that is readily available all seasons in this city.

Purchase your lavender at Home Depot for $35!
Home Depot Lavender
