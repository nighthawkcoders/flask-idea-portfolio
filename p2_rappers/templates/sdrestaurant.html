Restaurants in San Diego

Landini's Pizzeria

This is an Italian Restaurant that specializes in making pizza. This restaurant is very afforable with most meals ranging from $5 to $25.

Dija Mara

This is a restaurant located on the coastal area of San Diego. It is majorly influenced by South Asian cuisine but is infused with Californian culture as well.

Jeune et Jolie

This is a restaurant located in Carlsbad. This restaurant serves French cuisine. While it may seem very sophisticated, it does not serve overly fancy food and rather is more practical in its menu.

George's At the Cove

This restaurant is located in La Jolla. It is very unique in that you get to create your own meal. The restaurant has its own gastrobar and is very innovative in design. The food is of very high quality and chefs inspect each and every meal closely.

Sushi Tadokoro

This restaurant is located in San Diego Avenue. It serves mainly Japanese cuisine. This restaurant is popular for its strict technique in serving the meal and creating the food.
