Landmarks of San Diego

Mt. Soledad National Veterans Memorial

This is a tribute to the soldiers who risked their lives to fight in every war until now such as WWI, WWII, Vietnam War, Korean War, Civil War, and more.

Petco Park

Located in Downtown San Diego, Petco Park is home to the San Diego Padres baseball team.

Mission San Diego de Alcala

The mission at San Diego, the first of 21 mission along the California Coast, marks the birthplace of the sperad of Christianity on the West Coast of America.

Coronado Bridge

The Coronado Bridge was built in 1967 and cost 50 million dollars. This bridge is an astonishing 2.12 miles long

Old Town

Old Town marks the first spanish settlement on the West Coast of the United States. Created in 1769, Old Town now has many resturants and colorful shops.
