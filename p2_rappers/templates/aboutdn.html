Explore Del Norte High School

Welcome to Del Norte High School! This public school has been open since 2009. It's located in San Diego, California and is run by Poway Unified School District. The principal, Bryan Schultz, takes care of students from grades 9-12.

Map of the school
Classes

All the buildings in Del Norte link to the same area, which is the lunch area. There are multiple buildings that have a different letter for each building. The size of the campus is exactly 62 acres.

Here in Del Norte, there are plenty of classes students can take. For AP's there are options such as AP Computer Science (the best), APUSH, AP Psychology, AP Studio Art, and many more. Student's can not only take classes to help them in college, they can also take classes that fuels their personal interests.
